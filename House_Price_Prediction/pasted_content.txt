# House Price Prediction & Real Estate Intelligence Platform

Build a complete enterprise-grade AI-powered House Price Prediction and Real Estate Analytics Platform suitable for a Final Year Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Engineering Capstone Project. The application must look like a real-world commercial product developed by a professional software team and should be portfolio-worthy for placements, internships, and software engineering roles.

## Objective

Develop a responsive full-stack web application that predicts house prices using Machine Learning while providing advanced real estate insights, market trends, investment recommendations, location intelligence, and interactive visual analytics.

The application should demonstrate expertise in:
